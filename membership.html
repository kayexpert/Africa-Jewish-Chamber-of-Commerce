Membership

All people in Africa and Africans in the Diaspora who are interested in the vision, mission, aims, and objectives of the AJCC are welcome to join the AJCC Chamber. Where necessary, the Chamber also names Honorary Members.

You must love Israel and have faith in the future of Africa, starting in your neighborhood, in order to join the AJCC. African businesses who identify as Christians of all denominations and base their operations on Biblical principles are members of the AJCC.

Each successfully enrolled member will have access to the business profiles of both Israeli and African members as well as be listed in the AJCC Members' Directory of Goods and Services (database of business profiles of all registered members).

Unfortunately, applicants who do not pass the credibility check will not be able to register as members; however, they may reapply after the condition(s) that caused their disqualification improve.

Member Benefits

The AJCC has many and various programs and initiatives in mind, but they mostly depend on the wants and interests of their members. Typically, the Chamber would take the lead in organizing events like:

  • Training and capacity development for its members.
  • Organization, promotion and participation in trade fairs within and outside the continent.
  • Participation in trade and investment forum on specific subjects of interest to members.
  • Access different forms of capital.
  • Participation and support of programmes organized by the Israeli Embassies in the various African countries with diplomatic relations with these African countries.
  • Make friends with businesses of like-minded people and culture.
  • Learn business skills that sustain global corporations.
  • Connect faith and business to be a blessing to your community and the world.
  • Access to Business Advisory Services.
  • Access Business opportunities through the AJCC network, referral service and partnership with other business organizations.

  • Discounted rates on all AJCC Chamber services including trainings, workshops, trade fares etc.
