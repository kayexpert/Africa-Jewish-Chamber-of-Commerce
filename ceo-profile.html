Rev. Kofi Oduro Ofori-Agyeman-Prempeh.

Home About

The Founder, CEO & Chairman of the International Board of Trustees is in the person of Rev. Kofi Oduro Ofori-Agyeman-Prempeh. He was the founding CEO of the Ghana-Israel Chamber of Commerce which now operates by the name of Ghana Israel Business Chamber. Rev. Agyeman-Prempeh also founded the erstwhile twelve (12.000.000.00) million dollar AFRICAN DISASTER FUND amongst other organizations like the INTERNATIONAL CHRISTIAN ISRAELI BANK. Having founded and operated these organizations, he has a broad and wider perspective as a global player having once attended a World Bank international NGO financial workshop in Washington D. C with some heavy weight global business cum financial players. He has wide business dealings with his Israeli Counterparts, Partners and financiers.

Rev Agyeman Prempeh co-signed an Israeli Bi-National Chamber with the then Deputy Head of the Ghana Mission in Israel, His Excellency Y. S. A Amankwah, “AN AGREEMENT OF COOPERATION BETWEEN GHANA-ISRAEL CHAMBER OF COMMERCE & ISRAEL-AFRICA CHAMBER OF COMMERCE” It was signed at the then Ghanaian Embassy at 12 Ha’Chilazon Street, Ramat-Gan Israel, on the 18th day of December, 2009. As a potential global entrepreneur of decades of business collaborations, he has a network with several international Jewish entrepreneurs both in Israel and in the Jewish diaspora.

As a servant of the Jewish Moshiac Yeshua haMashiac whose servant he is and whom he serves as the National Director and West African Cordinator of the International Christian Embassy Jerusalem (ICEJ) based in the State of Israel and operates in over hundred countries of the world, his official duties would be of immense help to the fortunes of the African-Jewish Chamber of Commerce. As a minister of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, Rev. Agyeman-Prempeh continues to minister and serves as the Founder and General Overseer of MOUNT CALVARY DESTINY CHURCH INTERNATIONAL (LBG), formerlly called CALVARY MESSIANIC CHURCH (CMC), which name was changed, re-registered and re-incorporated on the 8th day of September 2021.

Rev Kofi Oduro Ofori-Agyeman-Prempeh is a proud Subscriber and a Voting member of the United Kingdom based Amnesty International but sharply differs on Amnesty International (AI). Nevertheless, he opposes Amnesty position and one sided stand against the State of Israel and vehemently opposes the almost eternal one sided vote against Israel. A case in point is the current one sided UN voting of the referral of the only genuine Middle Eastern democratic state to the International Court of Justice for an opinion of the so called occupation of the State of Israel of Judea & Samaria, anti-semitically and unjustifiably referred to as the West Bank. Rev.
